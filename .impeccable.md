## Design Context

### Users

CoderX serves developers and technical writers who browse, publish, and discuss programming articles. The homepage should quickly communicate the product's writing, reading, and AI-assisted authoring capabilities without slowing visitors down.

### Brand Personality

Retro, technical, and handmade. The interface should feel authored and exploratory rather than corporate, glossy, or generated from a generic component kit.

### Aesthetic Direction

Use restrained retro-computing typography and geometric background art, then contrast them with tactile paper details and hand-drawn annotations. Support both light and dark themes. Physical metaphors should follow believable lighting and material behavior; avoid decorative glassmorphism, exaggerated folds, and arbitrary irregular silhouettes.

### Design Principles

- Make each decorative effect explain a physical or interaction state.
- Prefer one memorable handcrafted detail over many generic effects.
- Keep text semantic, readable, and visually dominant over decoration.
- Use asymmetry deliberately while maintaining clear alignment and rhythm.
- Respect reduced motion and preserve the simpler unified mobile layout.
